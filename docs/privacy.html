Shutdown Club

Effective 2026-06-08

Privacy Policy

Shutdown Club is built around one promise: everything stays on your device. The app has no backend, makes no network requests for your data, and we operate no servers that could receive it.

The short version

  • There are no accounts and no sign-up. We never know who you are.
  • The app runs entirely offline. It does not send your data anywhere.
  • We use no third-party analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no trackers of any kind.
  • Your nights, scores, and Parking Lot notes are stored only in the app's local storage on your iPhone.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes everything it stored.

What we store, and where

When you use Shutdown Club, it saves the things you enter and the state of each night — your scheduled self-time, when you entered shutdown, your Parking Lot notes, your morning review, and the derived "Shutdown Score." This is kept in the app's private on-device database (SwiftData). It:

  • stays in the app's sandbox on your device;
  • is never transmitted to us or any third party;
  • is included in your personal iCloud or encrypted device backup only if you have those backups enabled — Apple controls that, not us;
  • is deleted when you delete the app, or when you choose Settings → Clear all data inside the app.

What we do not collect

Shutdown Club does not collect:

  • your name, email, phone number, or any device identifier;
  • precise or coarse location, IP address, or network telemetry;
  • analytics events, screen views, taps, or session data;
  • contacts, calendars, photos, microphone, camera, or health data.

We have no opinion on your sleep habits, because we never see them.

Notifications

If you grant permission, Shutdown Club schedules local-only notifications (an optional shutdown reminder and a gentle nudge beforehand). These are queued on your device by iOS. There is no push server — no remote endpoint can send you a message or learn that you received one.

Payments

Shutdown Club is a paid app. Payment is handled entirely by Apple’s App Store — there are no in-app purchases, and we never receive your payment details.

Children

Shutdown Club is not directed to children under 13, and because it collects nothing, it cannot knowingly process their data. There is nothing for us to delete server-side, because there is no server.

Changes to this policy

If the app ever changes what it stores or sends, we will update this page and bump the effective date above.
