Shutdown Club

Reclaim your evening

Not about sleeping earlier.

It's about not having to win yourself back at 1am. Shutdown Club helps you cash in a little freedom earlier in the evening, enter a calm shutdown window, and park what's still spinning — so the night stops being the only time that feels like yours.

It doesn't fix "I can't sleep." It helps with "I don't want to stop." Schedule a real block of your own time, enter shutdown, park the loose threads, and watch one number: did you open a new thread after shutdown?

Everything stays on your iPhone

No accounts. No servers. No analytics. No tracking. Your nights and your Parking Lot notes live only on your device and are deleted the moment you delete the app.
