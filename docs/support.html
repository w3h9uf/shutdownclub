Shutdown Club helps you stop opening new threads after midnight — schedule a real block of your own time, enter a shutdown window, park what's unfinished, and review it in the morning. Everything runs on your iPhone: no accounts, no servers, no analytics.

Common questions

Reminders aren't arriving.

Open the iPhone Settings → Notifications → Shutdown Club and make sure notifications are allowed. Inside the app, Settings → Shutdown reminder lets you set the time and toggle it on. Reminders are local to your device.

How do I start over?

Inside the app, Settings → Clear all data resets every night and Parking Lot note. There's also Load sample data if you'd like to see what Insights looks like after a couple of weeks.

Is my data private?

Yes — completely. Nothing leaves your phone. See the Privacy Policy for details.

What languages are supported?

Shutdown Club follows your device language, with English and Simplified Chinese (简体中文) built in.
